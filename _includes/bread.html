Bread glorious bread

Today I made some bread. I have rediscovered the joy of cooking recently and I'm not talking about the book. I bought a copy of Simple and Classic recipe book recently. I bought some yeast and it turned out to be incredibly straight forward to make the dough. So I decided to make some bread.

Here is the recipe:

  • 400g flour
  • 1 tsp quick yeast
  • 300ml luke warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt

Mix together the water and yeast, then add the flour and mix with a spoon till there are no clumpy bits on the edge of the bowl. Then put it onto a floured surface and knead with your hands until its smooth. Leave it for a few hours to rise in a bowl covered with a dishtowel. Finally knead and roll the douogh into a loaf and leave to rise for an hour or so. You can cut a cross into them with a small knife and put a bit of olive oil or butter ontop for some flavour. Finally bake in a 220 degrees fan oven for 30 mins
