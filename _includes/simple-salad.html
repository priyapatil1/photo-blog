a simple salad

It's much easier to eat healthily in the summer. Mostly because you don't crave hot comfort food. So I'm enjoying putting togethr salads for lunch. I forgoot how easy it is to put together a simple dressing.

Here is all you need:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

In this recipe I used Kale, some spinach and cherry tomatoes becase that's what I had lying around but you could use anything really.
